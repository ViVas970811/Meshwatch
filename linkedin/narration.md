## Delivery notes

* Read each block in one breath. The cadence reads natural at ~150 words per minute.
* Pause about 0.4 sec between blocks. This is exactly the transition time between screens in the auto-tour script.
* Smile on the last line. It changes how your voice lands without you having to act it.
* If you flub a block, just re-record that block. Clipchamp's audio recorder lets you stitch takes.
